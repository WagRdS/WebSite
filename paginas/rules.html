Basic Rules

A Poker Style Card Game

Welcome to the last defense against the invasion of the Chaos Leaders. In this game, each player receives five cards and has to form sets of heroes to face the hordes of Chaos Leaders. Just like in a Poker game, the player must form pairs, trips and other combinations to use the Heroes' abilities.

❱ Game Setup ❰

  1. Separate the hero cards, shuffle them and form a pile;
  2. Separate the opponents' cards by their backs, which contain a coin. They must be separated according to the value of each. The coins without values are the starting cards.
  3. Separate the Leaders by forming a line, in numerical order, according to the number of the coin on the back.
  4. Shuffle the other cards and place them on the corresponding leader.
  5. Form a pile with the opponent's cards, with the coin face up, and the highest value at the bottom.
  6. Arrange the initial cards in a 3 x 3 grid.
  7. Each player receives five hero cards.
  8. Each player will face a row of 3 cards. This line will be considered your first line, where most effects resolve. Each player will have a different first lines.

The player receives 5 cards and needs to make combinations between them to activate their heroes' abilities. Combinations such as pairs and triplets activate simpler abilities; Getting a sequence of four or five cards with one of a kind has a stronger effect.

❱ Gold and Damage ❰

  • During his turn, the player can pay 1 gold and discard up to two cards, drawing new ones. To spend gold, flip the coin face down on the top card of the Loot Pile, then place this card on the bottom of the pile.
  • When an opponent deals damage, take a damage card and place it in front of the player to form a Damage Pile. There is no limit to the damage a player can take, this game has no player elimination.

❱ Points ❰

  • At the end of game, Each coin in the Loot Pile is worth 2 points (1 point for the coin and another for the opponent on the back of the card);
  • Each face-up opponent in the Loot Pile is worth 1 point, except Chaos Leaders.
  • Each damage is subtracted from the final score.
  • Whoever scores the most points wins the game, in case of a draw, the winner is the one who beat the most leaders.
