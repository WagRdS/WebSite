Sets of Bard

The Bard, with the power of his music, can confuse his opponents.

  • Pair of Bard ➾ Change the position of a target with an adjacent one.
  • Triplets of Bard ➾ Move the first row sideways, shifting the target from the edge to the other side.
  • Four of Bard ➾ Move the first row sideways, shifting the target from the edge to the other side. Deal 1 damage to this target.
  • Five of Bard ➾ Move the first row sideways, shifting the target from the edge to the other side. Deal 2 damage to all targets in this row.
