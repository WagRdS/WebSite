Sets of Pirate

Like any good Captain, the Pirate knows how to recruit heroes to join in battle.

  • Pair of Pirates ➾ Draw 1 card.
  • Triplets of Pirates ➾ Draw 2 cards and deal 1 damage to a target in the first row.
  • Four of Pirates ➾ Draw 3 cards and deal 2 damage to a target in the first row.
  • Five of Pirates ➾ Draw 4 cards and deal 3 damage to a target in the first row.
