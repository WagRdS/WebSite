Sets of Mobs

Minions of the Leaders of Chaos, their role is to annihilate anyone who tries to stop their masters' plans.

At the end of the player's turn, the remaining minions in the first row deal damage, as described below.

  • Two level 1 minions deal 1 damage
  • Each level 2 minion deal 1 damage
  • Each level 3 minion deal 2 damage

Rules of Engagement

When a minion is defeated, its card is placed in the Reward Pile in front of the player with the coin facing up.

For a new opponent to enter, move one from the second row to the first row and place a new one in the open space. This rule applies when more than one opponent is defeated in one turn. Leaders always enter the center of the field.
