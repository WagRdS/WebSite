Sets of Warrior

A skilled combatant specialized in inflicting damage on his opponents.

  • Pair of Warriors ➾ Deal 1 damage to a target on the fist row.
  • Triplets of Warriors ➾ Deals 2 damage to a target, or, split the damage on the first row.
  • Four of Warriors ➾ Deal 3 damage to a target, or, split the damage on the first row.
  • Five of Warriors ➾ Deal 4 damage to a target, or, split the damage on the first row.
